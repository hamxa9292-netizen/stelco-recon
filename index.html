▦ RECONCILIATION SNAPSHOT

Adjustment Details

Generate the “Adjustment Details Accounts In Opening & Closing Debtors” file by diffing the prior-month closing debtors against the current-month opening debtors. Amounts are exact; reasons are auto-classified and low-confidence rows are flagged on a Review sheet.

▚ HOW THE ADJUSTMENT IS FOUND

Adjustment (2)

The closing-side plug: collection recorded that reduced no open debtor balance. Adj(2) = Closing − Opening(after adj) − Sales − Credits + Collection. Upload the collection ledger plus the opening & closing debtor detail; unabsorbed payments (prior-period bills that sit on neither ledger) are itemised. Enter the four control totals to check the itemised sum against the identity.

Statement totals

All four drive the plug. Leave them blank to itemise without the reconciliation check.
